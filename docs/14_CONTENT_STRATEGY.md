# Content Strategy

## Content Types
- Product pages
- Research pages
- Ingredient pages
- Market pages
- Blog articles
- FAQs
- Educational explainers

## Rules
- Write for users first.
- Support SEO without keyword stuffing.
- Keep claims compliant.
- Keep content aligned with the product and market context.

## Organization
Each content type should be mapped to a clear purpose, owner, and update rhythm.
