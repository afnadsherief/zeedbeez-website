# CAMERA.md

## Purpose
Define cinematic camera behavior for premium 3D storytelling.

## Camera Rules
- Camera movement must feel intentional.
- Use camera motion to reveal story beats.
- Avoid excessive shake or fast rotation.
- Use camera changes to support scroll transitions.

## Camera Modes
- Hero orbit
- Product reveal
- Research exploration
- Ingredient close-up
- Manufacturing process view

## Quality Rules
- Keep the camera stable enough for readability.
- Blend user influence subtly.
- Prioritize smooth interpolation.

## Fallback
Mobile experiences should simplify camera motion while preserving narrative order.
