# DESIGN.md

## Purpose
This is the premium design constitution for the ZeedBeez Enterprise Platform. It defines how the brand should feel, look, move, and present itself across all markets and products.

## Design Identity
ZeedBeez should feel like a premium biotechnology wellness brand that combines:
- scientific credibility
- luxury product presentation
- cinematic storytelling
- nature-inspired warmth
- modern editorial clarity

## Design Principles
- Every section must have a reason to exist.
- Every animation must support the story.
- Every visual treatment must increase trust, clarity, or desire.
- Avoid generic supplement-site aesthetics.
- Avoid visual noise.
- Avoid decorative complexity that does not improve the user experience.

## Visual Language
### Atmosphere
- Matte black foundations
- Deep charcoal layers
- Honey-gold highlights
- Soft glass reflections
- Premium shadow depth
- Controlled glow, not excess bloom

### Typography
- Large, authoritative headlines
- Highly readable body copy
- Clear hierarchy
- Short premium statements rather than long promotional blocks
- Scientific callouts should be distinct but restrained

### Layout
- Spacious sections
- Strong alignment
- Clear rhythm
- Editorial pacing
- Product-first visual focus

## 3D Art Direction
### Hero Bottle Presentation
- Glass must feel premium and realistic.
- Honey motion must feel smooth, rich, and viscous.
- Ingredient particles must feel intentional, not chaotic.
- Camera movement must feel cinematic and stable.
- Reflections and lighting should reinforce quality.

### Scene Standards
- Each scene must transition naturally into the next.
- Scene changes should feel like chapter changes in a premium film.
- The user should always understand where they are in the story.

## Motion Language
- Use scroll as a storytelling instrument.
- Use camera movement to reveal meaning.
- Use subtle micro-interactions to support product confidence.
- Avoid playful motion that weakens premium positioning.
- Keep reduced-motion support respectful and functional.

## Product Presentation Rules
### UAE Products
- HyperMoon
- Herbze

### India Products
- ZeedBeez Pro
- Sleep Better

Each product must have its own visual identity within the shared brand system.

## Market Design Rules
- The platform must support multiple markets without redesign.
- Regional product pages may adapt content and emphasis, but not the core brand system.
- The design system must remain consistent across markets.

## Accessibility Standards
- Clear contrast.
- Keyboard-accessible interactions.
- Visible focus states.
- Alt text and semantic structure.
- Reduced motion and performance fallbacks.

## Quality Bar
The final experience must feel:
- premium
- calm
- intelligent
- modern
- trustworthy
- memorable

## Anti-Patterns
- No cluttered supplement-page layouts.
- No cheap stock-style imagery.
- No exaggerated motion for its own sake.
- No weak hierarchy.
- No design choices that reduce trust.
